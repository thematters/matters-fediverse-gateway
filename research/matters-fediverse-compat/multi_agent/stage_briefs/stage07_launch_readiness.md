## Objective

整合 launch gate、測試矩陣、runbook 與下一輪工程包，讓後續 agent 可直接進入實作與驗證。

## Inputs

- `outputs/next-steps.md`
- `outputs/engineering-task-seeds.md`

## Outputs

- launch readiness handoff
- final execution plan
- prioritized engineering tasks
